Kevin D. Fujikawa, MD

We are dedicated to providing patients with high quality care and the commitment to improve and maintain the health of our patients.

Dr. Fujikawa graduated from Loma Linda University School of Medicine in 1985. He completed his residency at Loma Linda University and Riverside General Hospital in southern California and is board certified in family practice. After moving to northern California with his wife and daughter he joined the family practice group at MedClinic and was there for 12 years. He then went into private practice. In 2003, Dr. Fujikawa opened his current family practice office in Fair Oaks. He is a Hill Physicians provider and also accepts many private insurances.

Kristen Waggoner, PA-C

It has been very rewarding building lasting relationships with our patients and treating multiple generations within the same family. I feel privileged to be able to care for the health and well-being of our patients.

Kristen received her BS in Molecular, Cellular, and Developmental Biology at UCSB, then got her Masters of Physician Assistant Studies at Samuel Merritt University. Prior to joining the Fujikawa team, she worked in family practice and urgent care. Kristen has been working with Dr. Fujikawa since 2013. She enjoys baking and spending time with her family, puppy and kitties.
